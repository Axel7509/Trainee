Amogus is a character from the popular game Among Us.

Amogus Characteristics:

  • Color: Red
  • Role: Impostor
  • Description: Amogus is one of the playable characters in the game Among Us. He has a red color and can act as an Impostor, meaning a saboteur, or as a Crewmate, a member of the crew. Amogus has gained popularity as a character due to internet memes and meme phrases associated with the game Among Us.
